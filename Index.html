Hurdles

Cybersecurity has developed exponentially since it's first iteration as the Reaper that was created by Ray Tomlinson in order to get rid of Creeper that was printing the sentence "I'M THE CREEPER: CATCH ME IF YOU CAN."

That was over 5 decades ago. Now antivirus has developed to the point that it protects against credit card scams and leaks of important data.

More specifically the biggest issues that cybersecurity fixes/limits are as follows:

  • Software vulnerabilities
  • IoT attacks
  • Ransomware attacks
  • Cloud attacks
  • Phishing attacks
  • Blockchain attacks
  • AI attacks
  • Insider attacks

Social Impact

Cybersecurity has a giant impact in today's society because it is crucial in the fight against hackers.

Imagine a world in which hackers can steal all of your personal and important information and use that to ruin your life (Which already is happening).

Imagine now all of those hackers but in a colossal quantity and you had no defense at all to stop what the hackers did to get your information.

That is the world that cybersecurity never existed in.
